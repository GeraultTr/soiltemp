# Contributing to SoilTemp

Contributions are welcome, and they are greatly appreciated! Every little bit helps, and credit will always be given.

Please contribute by [reporting issues](https://github.com/baugetfa/soiltemp/issues), [submitting pull requests](https://github.com/baugetfa/soiltemp/pulls), or [providing feedback](https://github.com/baugetfa/soiltemp/issues).
